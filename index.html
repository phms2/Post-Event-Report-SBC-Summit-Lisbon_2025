Supplier Analysis

Feeders (Stand Builder)

Positive

  • Overall trust and autonomy were valued.
  • Excellent quality in the final finishes.
  • Quick resolution of unforeseen problems.

Negative

  • Initial setup issues (video, logo).
  • Little assistance the day before the event.
  • Serious air conditioning problem (poor planning).

Food Story (Catering)

Negative

  • Significant delays and delivery failures on the first day.

SMILE (Hosts)

Positive: Impeccable service.

To Improve: Need for 2-3 additional hosts.

SBC (Organization)

Negative

  • App failure for badge scanning, preventing lead capture as planned.
  • Inadequate A/C in the Affiliate area (tent) on Day 1, making conditions unbearable. It improved slightly on Day 2 after complaints.

Key Conclusions & Recommendations

Strengths to Replicate

  • Massive Team Presence: Success in distributing 100+ VIP passes.
  • Branding on Stages: Maximum brand exposure on Stage 4.
  • Industry Recognition: Dual award win validated our culture and product.
  • High-Level Networking: VIP Box was effective for strengthening client relationships.

Recommendations for the Future

  • Booth Functionality: Prioritize the Commercial department's suggestions for the next design.
  • Capitalize on Sponsorship: Develop strategies for the Commercial team to better leverage the Platinum status.
  • Supplier Contingency Plan: Require detailed plans and have alternatives.
  • Lead Capture Autonomy: Implement our own solution for badge scanning.
